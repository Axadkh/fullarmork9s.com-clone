ABOUT US

It is my Honor and Privilege to introduce Full Armor K9s, LLC out of Inman, SC. Specializing in Czech/European German Shepherds Breeding and Training. Protection, Passive Scent Tracking, and overall obedience is our passion and focus! We train ALL breeds of dogs in Obedience, and offer free evaluations for handlers who are interested in Tracking and Protection Services.
Owned and Operated by an OEF/OIF Veteran, these animals have become a part of my life and family. The finest balance of temperament, working drive, and sport ability throughout the Pedigree! This is a managed bloodline to maintain the quality of the offspring, not the quantity
